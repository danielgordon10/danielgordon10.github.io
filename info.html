ABOUT ME

For the last three years, I have worked at Third Wave Automation, developing visual algorithms for autonomous forklifts and managing a team of 5+ engineers. My Ph.D. work focussed on perception for robotics applications. Most recently, I worked on unsupervised learning from large video datasets. Previously, I focused on visual environment understanding for robots using simulation environments and a combination of reinforcement learning and supervised learning. Before that, I used recurrent neural networks for real-time object tracking in videos.
